## Notes for review

- Mowing is the only "hard" task — 30 pts feels right since it's a 30-60 min job depending on lot size.
- Watering is daily because skipping a day in July kills the garden. Parents on auto-irrigation can deactivate it.
- Pet waste chore is opt-in — if the family doesn't have a dog the parent can skip it at apply-time.
- No pool chore — too rare and equipment-specific. Custom-add if needed.
