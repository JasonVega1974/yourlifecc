## Chores

| # | Name | Category | Difficulty | Points | Recurring | Emoji |
|---|------|----------|------------|--------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Help decorate (tree / lights / wreath) | family | medium | 15 | once | 🎁 |
| 2 | Wrap gifts (5+ items) | family | easy | 10 | once | 🎀 |
| 3 | Write 3 thank-you notes | personal | easy | 10 | once | ✉️ |
| 4 | Tidy guest room / bathroom | cleaning | easy | 10 | weekly | 🛌 |
| 5 | Bake or prep one dish for a family meal | kitchen | medium | 20 | once | 🍪 |
| 6 | Help set the holiday table | family | easy | 10 | once | 🍽️ |
| 7 | Take down + box decorations (end of season) | family | medium | 15 | once | 📦 |

**Pack total:** 90 points

## Notes for review

- Thank-you notes is intentionally personal-category, not family — it's a kid-skill (writing, gratitude practice), not a household task.
- "Help decorate" is one chore, not three, because in practice it's a single afternoon. If a family does outdoor lights separately the parent can add a custom one.
- #7 is the only chore that triggers at end-of-season (early January). Worth noting at apply-time so it doesn't read as "due now" in November.
- No religious-tradition-specific chores in the default pack — keep it inclusive. Christmas-specific items can be added by parents who want them.
