QUESTIONS
Common Questions
How does my friend get the $10 discount? ▼
When they click your referral link, the discount is automatically applied to their checkout — they don't need to enter any code. It works on all plans: monthly, annual, and lifetime.
When do I get paid? ▼
After your friend's purchase clears our 30-day refund window, we send you $10 via Venmo or PayPal (your choice). Payouts are processed on the 1st of each month for the prior month's confirmed referrals.
Is there a limit to how many families I can refer? ▼
No limit at all. Refer 2 families or 20 — you earn $10 for every single one. The more you share, the more you earn.
What plans count for the reward? ▼
Any paid plan — monthly ($9.99/mo), annual ($79.99/yr), or lifetime ($149 once). Free plan signups do not count. Your friend must complete a paid purchase for both rewards to apply.
Can I refer family members who live in the same household? ▼
No — referrals must be to separate households with separate accounts. Referring yourself, a spouse, or someone who shares your household is not allowed and will not be credited.
What's the difference between this and the Affiliate Program? ▼
The Referral Program is for existing YourLife CC customers sharing with friends — casual, word-of-mouth sharing. The Affiliate Program is for people who want to actively promote YourLife CC to a wider audience (social media, churches, communities) as a commission-only side income. Affiliates earn the same $10 per signup but have no limit on scale and can promote to any audience.
I don't see my referral link — where do I find it? ▼
Sign in to your account at yourlifecc.com/login and look for the "Referrals" tab in your account menu. If you don't see it yet, the feature may still be rolling out — email us at info@kingdom-creatives.com and we'll get you your link manually.

📋 REFERRAL PROGRAM RULES & TERMS

1

Who can participate: Any active YourLife CC customer with a paid account (monthly, annual, or lifetime) may participate in the referral program.

2

How your reward works: You earn $10 cash for each new unique customer who signs up for a paid plan using your personal referral link. Paid via Venmo or PayPal on the 1st of each month for referrals confirmed in the prior month.

3

How their discount works: The referred family receives $10 off their first payment automatically when they sign up through your link. This discount cannot be combined with other promotions.

4

30-day hold: Referral rewards are held for 30 days to cover the refund window. If your referred customer requests a refund, the reward is cancelled.

5

No self-referrals: You may not refer yourself, a family member in the same household, or any account you control. Violations result in reward forfeiture.

6

Unique customers only: Each referred person must be a brand-new YourLife CC customer. Existing users or previously cancelled accounts do not qualify.

7

No paid advertising: This referral program is for personal, organic sharing — word of mouth, text, email, social posts. Running paid ads with your referral link is not permitted under this program (see the Affiliate Program instead).

8

No limit on referrals: There is no cap on how many families you can refer or how much you can earn.

9

Program changes: YourLife CC reserves the right to modify or end the referral program at any time with 30 days notice to participants.
