Privacy Policy

YourLife.cc ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

⚠️ COPPA Notice: YourLife.cc is designed for use by families with parental supervision. Children under 13 may only use this service with verifiable parental consent. Parents create and manage accounts on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account and use the app:

  • Account information: Email address and password (encrypted) used to create your account
  • App data: Information you enter into the app such as goals, school grades, chores, health logs, journal entries, and other personal productivity data
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (pages visited, features used) to improve the service

We do not collect social security numbers, payment card numbers (Stripe handles payments separately), precise location data, or biometric data.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the YourLife.cc service
  • To sync your data across devices via our secure cloud database
  • To send account-related emails (password resets, confirmations)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

🔒 We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers. Your family's data belongs to your family.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant database platform hosted on AWS. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use row-level security so users can only access their own data.

Despite our security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

PARENTAL RIGHTS (COPPA)

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review the personal information collected about their child
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
  • Export all data stored in their account

To exercise these rights, visit your Account page or contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile data is immediately removed from our database. Backups may retain data for up to 30 days before being purged.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing, if applicable (privacy policy). We never see or store your full card number.
  • Vercel — web hosting (privacy policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery

COOKIES

We use minimal cookies and browser storage (localStorage) only to keep you signed in and save your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access: You can export all your data from your Account page at any time
  • Correction: You can update your information directly in the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from your Account page
  • Portability: Data exports are provided in JSON format

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
