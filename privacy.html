Privacy Policy

YourLifeCC.com ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. YourLifeCC.com is a product of Kingdom Creatives LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Idaho, USA.

📋 KEY POINTS — QUICK SUMMARY

Who can use this app: Adults 18+ only may create accounts. Children use the app through a parent-created account. Children under 13 require verified parental consent under COPPA.

What we collect: Your email address, child profile data you enter, and app usage data. We never collect payment card numbers, GPS location, biometrics, or photos.

What we never do: We never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models.

Your data rights: You can access, correct, export, or permanently delete all your data at any time by emailing info@kingdom-creatives.com.

Communications: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email. SMS requires separate opt-in and can be cancelled by replying STOP.

Children's data: Child profile data is visible only to you. We never share it with sponsors, schools, churches, or any third party without your explicit consent.

Questions? Email info@kingdom-creatives.com — we respond within 30 days, or within 48 hours for urgent child safety matters.

⚠️ COPPA Notice: YourLifeCC.com is designed for use by families under active parental supervision. Our service is directed at parents and guardians — not at children acting independently.

Verifiable Parental Consent (VPC): Under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 16 C.F.R. Part 312, operators must obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13. YourLifeCC.com satisfies this requirement through its account structure: only adults may create accounts, and a parent or guardian must affirmatively check an explicit consent acknowledgment when adding any child profile. This acknowledgment — which constitutes VPC under COPPA — is recorded with a date and timestamp in our system and is available upon request.

COPPA §312.5 compliance: In accordance with 16 C.F.R. §312.5, we provide parents and guardians with the right to: (a) review the personal information collected from their child; (b) direct us to delete that information; (c) refuse further collection or use of their child's information; and (d) agree to the collection of their child's information while prohibiting its disclosure to third parties. Child data is never disclosed to third parties, sponsors, advertisers, or other users without explicit parental consent. Children do not create their own accounts and cannot use the Service without being added by a parent-created account.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without this documented VPC mechanism. If you believe we have inadvertently received data from a child under 13 outside this framework, contact us immediately at info@kingdom-creatives.com and we will delete it within 48 hours.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information provided directly when you create an account and use the app:

  • Parent account information: Email address and password (encrypted via Supabase Auth) used to create your account
  • Child profile data: Names, ages, and profile settings entered by the parent when creating child profiles. Parents control all child profile creation and configuration.
  • App data entered by parents: Goals, behavioral notes, chore assignments, schedules, school grade inputs, Parent Hub settings, and other parent-managed configuration data
  • App data entered directly by children: Certain sections of YourLifeCC.com allow children to interact directly with the app under parental supervision. This includes journal entries, quiz responses, chore completion check-offs, goal progress updates, and similar self-directed inputs. This data is stored in the parent's account and is accessible only to the parent account holder.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (sections visited, features used) to improve the service. This is non-identifiable aggregate data.
  • Error and diagnostic data: Technical error reports via Sentry to help us identify and fix app issues. These reports contain technical diagnostic data (error type, browser, device, page URL) only — not your personal app content such as journal entries, grades, or child profile data.

We do not collect social security numbers, payment card numbers (Stripe handles payments separately and we never see your full card number), precise GPS location data, biometric data, photos or images, or any data from children who have not been added to a parent-created account.

🔒 We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes. Your family's data belongs to your family.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored and processed primarily in the United States. By using the Service from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence. Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II compliant database platform hosted on AWS. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We use row-level security (RLS) policies so that authenticated users can only access their own data — no user can read another family's records, even with a valid session token.

Despite our security measures, no internet-based system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, your profile data and all associated app data — including all child profile data — are immediately flagged for deletion and removed from active databases within 24 hours. Encrypted backup snapshots may retain data for up to 30 days before being permanently purged. We do not retain data beyond 30 days following account deletion.

Marketing suppression records (unsubscribe and SMS opt-out records) are retained indefinitely even after account deletion, as required by law, to ensure you are not re-added to marketing communications in the future.

The sensitive nature of waitlist applications — which may include descriptions of financial hardship or family circumstances — is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. This data is accessed only by Kingdom Creatives LLC staff directly involved in program administration and is never used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than matching and program operation.
