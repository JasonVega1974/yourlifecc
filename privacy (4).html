Privacy Policy

YourLife.cc ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. YourLife.cc is a product of Kingdom Creatives LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Idaho, USA.

⚠️ COPPA Notice: YourLife.cc is designed for use by families under active parental supervision. Our service is directed at parents and guardians — not at children acting independently.

How parental consent works in YourLife.cc: Only adults may create a YourLife.cc account. During account creation and again when adding a child profile, the parent or guardian must affirmatively acknowledge that they are the legal parent or guardian of each child they add and that they consent to that child's use of the service. This acknowledgment is recorded with a timestamp. Children do not create their own accounts and cannot use the service without being added by a parent-created account.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without this documented parental consent mechanism. If you believe we have inadvertently received data from a child under 13 outside this framework, contact us immediately at info@kingdom-creatives.com and we will delete it within 48 hours.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information provided directly when you create an account and use the app:

  • Parent account information: Email address and password (encrypted via Supabase Auth) used to create your account
  • Child profile data: Names, ages, and profile settings entered by the parent when creating child profiles. Parents control all child profile creation and configuration.
  • App data entered by parents: Goals, behavioral notes, chore assignments, schedules, school grade inputs, Parent Hub settings, and other parent-managed configuration data
  • App data entered directly by children: Certain sections of YourLife.cc allow children to interact directly with the app under parental supervision. This includes journal entries, quiz responses, chore completion check-offs, goal progress updates, and similar self-directed inputs. This data is stored in the parent's account and is accessible only to the parent account holder.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (sections visited, features used) to improve the service. This is non-identifiable aggregate data.
  • Error and diagnostic data: Technical error reports via Sentry to help us identify and fix app issues. These reports contain technical diagnostic data (error type, browser, device, page URL) only — not your personal app content such as journal entries, grades, or child profile data.

We do not collect social security numbers, payment card numbers (Stripe handles payments separately and we never see your full card number), precise GPS location data, biometric data, photos or images, or any data from children who have not been added to a parent-created account.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the YourLife.cc service
  • To sync your data securely across devices via our cloud database
  • To send account-related emails (activation links, password resets, service updates)
  • To send onboarding and feature emails to help you get value from the app (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

🔒 We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes. Your family's data belongs to your family.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored and processed primarily in the United States. By using the Service from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence. Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II compliant database platform hosted on AWS. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We use row-level security (RLS) policies so that authenticated users can only access their own data — no user can read another family's records, even with a valid session token.

Despite our security measures, no internet-based system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

PARENTAL RIGHTS (COPPA)

Parents and guardians have the following rights with respect to their children's data:

  • Review all personal information collected about their child at any time by logging into the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information without deleting the parent account
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child's information by removing the child profile
  • Export all data stored in their account in JSON format

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@kingdom-creatives.com. We will respond within 30 days. For urgent child safety or data matters, we will respond within 48 hours.

AGE REQUIREMENTS & CONSENT FRAMEWORK UPDATED

✅ How consent is established and recorded:

1. Account creation: Only adults (18+) may create a YourLife.cc account. By creating an account, you represent that you are an adult.

2. Child profile creation: When adding a child profile, the parent must check an explicit acknowledgment: "I am the parent or legal guardian of this child and I consent to their use of YourLife.cc under my supervision." This acknowledgment is recorded with a date and timestamp in our system.

3. Children under 13: YourLife.cc is primarily designed for families with children ages 13 and older. If you add a child profile for a child under 13, the consent acknowledgment at profile creation serves as your verifiable parental consent under COPPA. The data practices and protections in this policy apply with full force to children of all ages.

4. Child login: Child profiles use a PIN-based login set by the parent — not an independent email or password — reinforcing that child access is always mediated through the parent account.

If you are a parent who did not set up a child's profile but believe your child is using YourLife.cc, contact us at info@kingdom-creatives.com immediately. We will help you locate and, if desired, delete the associated data.

⚠️ Important notice for parents: YourLife.cc provides organizational and accountability tools. The manner in which these tools are applied within your household is solely your responsibility. If you have concerns about a child's wellbeing, please contact a qualified professional. If a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services (911) or the Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline at 1-800-422-4453.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, your profile data and all associated app data — including all child profile data — are immediately flagged for deletion and removed from active databases within 24 hours. Encrypted backup snapshots may retain data for up to 30 days before being permanently purged. We do not retain data beyond 30 days following account deletion.

DATA DELETION REQUESTS

You have the right to request complete deletion of all personal data associated with your account at any time, regardless of your subscription status. To submit a deletion request:

  • Email info@kingdom-creatives.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your account
  • We will confirm deletion within 30 days

California residents may also exercise rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of sale (we do not sell data). Contact us at the email above to exercise CCPA rights.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and cloud storage. Your app data including child profile data is stored here. (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing for parent subscription billing only. No child data is transmitted to Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your full payment card number. (privacy policy)
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — transactional and onboarding email delivery to parent accounts. Your parent email address is stored in Brevo to facilitate account communications. Child data is not transmitted to Brevo. (privacy policy)
  • Vercel — web hosting and content delivery. Standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) are retained per Vercel's data retention policy. (privacy policy)
  • Sentry — error monitoring and crash reporting. Error reports contain technical diagnostic data only (error type, browser, device, page URL) — not your personal app content. (privacy policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery. Google may log your IP address when fonts are loaded. This applies to all visitors.

We do not authorize any of these third parties to use your data for their own marketing purposes. We do not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking pixels, or data brokers.

COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

We use minimal cookies and browser localStorage only to keep you signed in and save your app preferences locally. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics cookies. You may clear your browser's local storage at any time, which will log you out of the app. Your data will automatically re-sync from our cloud database when you sign back in.

CHILDREN'S DATA — ADDITIONAL PROTECTIONS

Because YourLife.cc is used by families and involves data about minors, we apply the following additional protections beyond standard COPPA compliance:

  • Child profile data (names, ages, behavioral notes, academic records, journal entries, quiz responses) is accessible only to the parent account that created it — not to other users, not to Kingdom Creatives staff in normal operations
  • We do not use child data for any purpose other than delivering the Service to the parent account holder
  • We do not build behavioral profiles, advertising segments, or analytics models on minor users
  • We do not display advertising to any user, including minors
  • Child-entered data (journal entries, quiz responses, chore check-offs) is treated with the same confidentiality protections as parent-entered data and is never accessed by Kingdom Creatives staff except in response to a direct parent request or legal obligation
  • Child data is deleted in full when a parent account is deleted, or upon a specific child profile deletion request
  • We do not share child data with schools, churches, sponsors, or any third party without explicit parent consent

SPONSOR A FAMILY PROGRAM — DATA HANDLING

YourLife.cc operates a voluntary "Sponsor a Family" program. If you apply to our family sponsorship waitlist or make a sponsor payment, the following additional data practices apply:

Waitlist applicants (families seeking sponsorship):

  • We collect your name, email address, number of children, ages of children, church or community affiliation, and a description of why sponsorship would benefit your family
  • We may also collect the name and email of a person who referred you or indicated they would sponsor you specifically
  • This information is used solely for matching your application with an available sponsor, communicating about your waitlist status, and administering your sponsored access once matched
  • We do not share your personal story, financial situation, or application details with sponsors beyond confirming that a match was made and access was activated
  • Waitlist application data is retained until your application is fulfilled, withdrawn, or the program is discontinued. You may request deletion at any time by emailing info@kingdom-creatives.com

Sponsors (individuals or organizations making sponsor payments):

  • We collect your name and email address to process your payment and confirm your match
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe — we do not store your full card number
  • If you designated a specific family to sponsor, we retain that information to facilitate the match
  • We will confirm when a family has been matched and activated — we will not share the matched family's personal information with you

The sensitive nature of waitlist applications — which may include descriptions of financial hardship or family circumstances — is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. This data is accessed only by Kingdom Creatives LLC staff directly involved in program administration and is never used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than matching and program operation.
