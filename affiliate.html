📋 AFFILIATE PROGRAM RULES & TERMS

1

Eligibility: You must be 18 or older to participate. Employees and immediate family members of YourLife CC / Kingdom Creatives are not eligible.

2

Referral Codes: Each affiliate receives one unique referral code issued by YourLife CC. Codes are non-transferable and may not be shared with another affiliate for credit-splitting purposes.

3

How Sales Are Tracked: A sale is credited to you when a new customer enters your referral code at checkout. The code must be entered at the time of purchase — we cannot apply codes retroactively.

4

Commission Amounts: Monthly plan = $5. Annual plan = $10. Lifetime plan = $20. Commissions are one-time per unique customer. You do not earn recurring commissions on monthly renewals.

Sponsor & Donor Bonuses: When you land a church, school, or organization that sponsors multiple family subscriptions, you earn a flat bonus in addition to individual commissions: 5–14 families = $50, 15–29 families = $100, 30–49 families = $150, 50+ families = $150 + 10% of total sponsor payment. Sponsor bonuses are paid after 30-day retention confirmation.

5

Payment Schedule: Commissions are paid on the 15th of each month for sales confirmed in the prior month. Minimum payout threshold is $20. Unpaid balances roll to the next month.

6

Refund Policy: If a referred customer receives a refund within 30 days, the associated commission will be reversed. Commissions are not paid until the refund window has closed.

7

Self-Referrals Prohibited: You may not use your own referral code to purchase a plan for yourself, a family member, or any account you control. Violations will result in immediate termination.

8

Honest Promotion: You may not make false, misleading, or exaggerated claims about YourLife CC. All promotional content must be truthful and comply with FTC disclosure requirements (you must disclose your affiliate relationship).

9

Approved Channels: Promotion via social media, email, church networks, blogs, and word of mouth is encouraged. Paid advertising using YourLife CC trademarks requires written pre-approval. Spam or unsolicited bulk messaging is strictly prohibited.

10

Program Modification & Termination: YourLife CC reserves the right to change commission rates, rules, or terminate the program at any time with 30 days notice. We may terminate an individual affiliate's account at any time for violation of these terms.

11

No Guarantee of Earnings: This is a commission-only program. No earnings are guaranteed. Results depend entirely on your promotional effort and audience.
