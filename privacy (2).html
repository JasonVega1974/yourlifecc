Privacy Policy

YourLife.cc ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. YourLife.cc is a product of Kingdom Creatives LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Idaho, USA.

⚠️ COPPA Notice: YourLife.cc is designed for use by families with parental supervision. Children under 13 may only use this service with verifiable parental consent. Parents create and manage accounts on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13 without consent, contact us immediately at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account and use the app:

  • Account information: Email address and password (encrypted via Supabase Auth) used to create your account
  • App data: Information you enter into the app such as goals, school grades, chores, health logs, journal entries, child profiles, behavioral notes, and other personal productivity data
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (pages visited, features used) to improve the service
  • Error and diagnostic data: Technical error reports (via Sentry) to help us identify and fix app issues. These reports do not contain your personal app data.

We do not collect social security numbers, payment card numbers (Stripe handles payments separately), precise GPS location data, biometric data, or any data from children without a parent-created account.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the YourLife.cc service
  • To sync your data securely across devices via our cloud database
  • To send account-related emails (activation links, password resets, service updates)
  • To send onboarding and feature emails to help you get value from the app (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

🔒 We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes. Your family's data belongs to your family.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored and processed primarily in the United States. By using the Service from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence. Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II compliant database platform hosted on AWS. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We use row-level security (RLS) policies so that authenticated users can only access their own data — no user can read another family's records, even with a valid session token.

Despite our security measures, no internet-based system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

PARENTAL RIGHTS (COPPA)

Parents and guardians have the following rights with respect to their children's data:

  • Review the personal information collected about their child at any time
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
  • Export all data stored in their account in JSON format

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days. For urgent child safety matters, we will respond within 48 hours.

⚠️ Important notice for parents: YourLife.cc provides organizational and accountability tools. The manner in which these tools are applied within your household is solely your responsibility. If you have concerns about a child's wellbeing, please contact a qualified professional. If a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services (911) or the Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline at 1-800-422-4453.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, your profile data and all associated app data are immediately flagged for deletion and removed from active databases within 24 hours. Encrypted backup snapshots may retain data for up to 30 days before being permanently purged. We do not retain data beyond 30 days following account deletion.

DATA DELETION REQUESTS NEW

You have the right to request complete deletion of all personal data associated with your account at any time, regardless of your subscription status. To submit a deletion request:

  • Email [email protected] with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your account
  • We will confirm deletion within 30 days

California residents may also exercise rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of sale (we do not sell data). Contact us at the email above to exercise CCPA rights.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and cloud storage (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy). We never see, store, or have access to your full payment card number.
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — transactional and onboarding email delivery (privacy policy). Your email address is stored in Brevo to facilitate account communications.
  • Vercel — web hosting and content delivery (privacy policy)
  • Sentry — error monitoring and crash reporting (privacy policy). Error reports contain technical diagnostic data only, not your personal app content.
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery. Google may log your IP address when fonts are loaded.

We do not authorize any of these third parties to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

We use minimal cookies and browser localStorage only to keep you signed in and save your app preferences locally. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics cookies. You may clear your browser's local storage at any time, which will log you out of the app. Your data will automatically re-sync from our cloud database when you sign back in.

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you by contacting [email protected]
  • Correction: Update your information directly in the app at any time
  • Deletion: Request full account and data deletion as described above
  • Portability: Request a data export in JSON format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data while a dispute is resolved
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes

CHILDREN'S DATA — ADDITIONAL PROTECTIONS NEW

Because YourLife.cc is used by families and may involve data about minors, we apply the following additional protections beyond standard COPPA compliance:

  • Child profile data (names, ages, behavioral notes, academic records) is accessible only to the parent account that created it
  • We do not use child data for any purpose other than delivering the Service to the parent account holder
  • We do not build behavioral profiles or analytics on minor users
  • We do not display advertising to any user, including minors
  • Child data is deleted in full when a parent account is deleted
