Reading Plans

📖 What's Inside

Everything you'd expect — and a few things you wouldn't.

Six features that turn casual Bible time into a daily rhythm. Free forever for the Faith Community.

🏛️

Biblical Archaeology

Real sites, real ruins, real photos. Walk through Jericho, Capernaum, the Pool of Bethesda — and see how the Bible meets the ground beneath it.

🃏

Flashcards

Bible facts, names, places, and verses on a spaced-repetition deck. The fastest way to actually remember what you read instead of forgetting it Tuesday.

🎵

Worship Playlist

A curated, in-app worship player. Set the tone before reading; carry the songs with you through the day. No leaving the app, no ads, no noise.

🙏

Prayer Journal

A private, personal place to write prayers, track answers, and look back at what God has done over weeks and months. The receipts of a praying life.

Bible Timeline

From Creation to Christ's Return, plotted on a single timeline. See how the prophets, kings, exiles, gospels, and Acts fit together at a glance.

Memory Verses

A built-in memorization system with SM-2 spaced repetition. Hide words, type them back, and have the verse stick — not just for the test, for the year.
