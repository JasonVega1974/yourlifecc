💭 Why Free?

Because the Gospel isn't for sale.

I'm a Christian dad in Idaho who built this for one simple reason: tools to grow in faith shouldn't be locked behind a subscription. Not for a teen who can't afford $70 a year. Not for a family on a tight budget. Not for anyone.

"Freely you have received; freely give." — Matthew 10:8

The other Christian apps charge $40-70 a year for what's in this app. Hallow, Glorify, Abide — they all gate the good stuff behind a paywall. That's their choice and I respect it. But I wanted something different: a complete, no-strings Christian app that anyone can use, today, with nothing held back.

If this app helps you grow closer to Jesus, that's the win. If you ever want to support the mission, there's an optional donation below. But you'll never be asked, nagged, or guilt-tripped. The app is yours.
