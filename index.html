❓ FAQ

Common Questions

What age range is this for? ▼

Ages 12-25+. Six life stages — Middle School through Young Adult. Content adapts automatically, showing age-appropriate lessons and features at each stage.

Is there a free trial? ▼

Yes — 3 days free on all plans. Full access to navigation, dashboard, tools, and 2 sample lessons per category. Complete lesson library, quizzes, certificates, and career profiles unlock with your paid subscription. No credit card required to start.

What's the difference between annual and lifetime? ▼

Annual is $29.99/year (Founding Family price, regular $49.99) and renews yearly. Lifetime is a one-time payment of $149.99 — pay once, access forever, including all future updates and new lessons.

How does the parent dashboard work? ▼

It's PIN-protected inside the app. Parents see a Life Score (weighted grade across academics, responsibility, growth, wellbeing, character, engagement), behavior logs, grade monitor, chore verification, incentives, and weekly reports — without accessing the child's private journal or mood entries.

Is my child's data private? ▼

Each license creates a private instance at a unique URL. Data is encrypted and stored in their secure account. No one else can access it. Church/org admins see only aggregate stats, never individual data.

How do church/org licenses work? ▼

Purchase a license, get an admin dashboard, and generate unique codes for each youth member. Students enter their code at yourlifecc.com to create their account. QR code posters make onboarding instant. Admin sees aggregate engagement — never individual private data. Codes can be issued, revoked, or reassigned anytime.

Can students earn money by referring friends? ▼

Yes! Students earn $5 for every friend who signs up through their referral link. Earnings are credited to their account and paid out at age 18+ or applied as account credit. Parents earn $10 per referral. Church partners earn 20% commission.

What devices does it work on? ▼

Any device with a browser — phones, tablets, laptops, desktops. No app store download. Just log in.

Is this faith-based? ▼

YourLife CC includes an optional Faith & Purpose category. The other 17 categories cover universal life skills. Faith content is clearly labeled and entirely optional.

What if I'm not satisfied? ▼

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email [email protected] and we'll refund you immediately.
