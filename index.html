❓ FAQ

Common Questions

What age range is this for? ▼

Ages 12–25+. Six life stages — Middle School through Young Adult. Content adapts automatically at each stage.

Is there a free plan? ▼

Yes — the free plan includes the core dashboard, school tracker, basic money tools, journal, goals, and 1 child profile. Paid plans unlock all 28 sections, unlimited children, and the full lesson library.

What's the difference between monthly and lifetime? ▼

Monthly is $9.99/month (or $79.99/year) and renews automatically. Lifetime is a one-time payment of $149 — pay once, access forever, including all future updates.

How does the parent dashboard work? ▼

It's PIN-protected inside the app. Parents see a Life Score, behavior logs, chore verification, grade monitor, screen time controls, and weekly reports — without accessing the child's private journal entries.

Is my child's data private? ▼

Yes. Each account is private and encrypted. Your unique URL is yours alone — no one else can access your family's data.

What devices does it work on? ▼

Any device with a browser — phones, tablets, laptops, desktops. No app download required. Just log in from yourlifecc.vercel.app.

Is this faith-based? ▼

YourLife CC includes an optional Faith & Purpose section with 365 scriptures, 30 devotionals, and Bible content. A Faith Mode toggle lets you turn it ON or OFF. The other 17 categories cover universal life skills for all families.

What if I'm not satisfied? ▼

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email [email protected] and we'll refund you immediately.
