❓ FAQ

Common Questions

What do I get during the 3-day trial? ▼

Full access to everything — all 28 sections, all 140+ lessons, the complete Parent Hub, chores & rewards, Life Score, every feature. No limitations, no credit card. After 3 days you pick the plan that fits your family or you stop — your choice.

What happens when my trial expires? ▼

After 3 days you'll be prompted to choose a plan — Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime. Your data is saved and waiting for you. If you don't pick a plan the app pauses until you do. We'll send a reminder before it expires so you're never caught off guard.

What's the real difference between Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime? ▼

All three plans give your family identical full access — every section, unlimited kids, Parent Hub, everything. The only difference is how you pay. Monthly is $9.99/month. Yearly is $79.99/year ($40 savings). Lifetime is $149 once — no more payments ever, plus all future updates included free. Break-even on Lifetime vs monthly is 15 months.

How do multiple kids work? ▼

Every child gets their own 4-digit PIN and completely separate dashboard — their data, their progress, their privacy. Switching between kids is instant. The Parent Hub shows you all of them in one place. All paid plans include unlimited children at no extra charge.

What ages is this designed for? ▼

Ages 11 through 22+. YourLife CC has four life stages — Middle School, High School, College, and Young Adult. Content adapts automatically at each stage. The app genuinely grows with your child for years, not months.

Can I cancel and get a refund? ▼

Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel anytime — no fees, no penalties, drops to paused at next billing cycle. Annual subscribers can cancel before renewal for a full refund. Lifetime plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee — email us and we'll refund every penny.

Is this faith-based? ▼

YourLife CC includes an optional Faith & Purpose section with 365 scriptures, 30 devotionals, and Bible content. A Faith Mode toggle lets families turn it ON or OFF. The other sections cover universal life skills for any family regardless of faith background.

What devices does it work on? ▼

Any device with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop. No app download required. Everything saves automatically. Your data is private and encrypted.
