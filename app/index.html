❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Is "Free" actually free — is there a catch? ▼

Actually free. The free tier gives one child access to the personal dashboard, school tracker, habit tracker, journal, money basics, and goals — permanently. We make money when families upgrade, not by trapping them.

Will I be charged automatically? ▼

No. The free plan requires no card at all. If you choose Monthly or Yearly, you only get charged when you enter payment details and confirm. Lifetime is a one-time checkout — no subscription, no renewal, no auto-charge. You will never be surprised by a charge on YourLife CC.

What's the difference between Monthly and Lifetime? ▼

Monthly is $9.99/month — full access for as long as you pay, cancel anytime. Lifetime is $149 once — same full access forever, including every future update. Break-even is 15 months. A family with kids under 15 today will almost certainly save money going Lifetime.

What age range is this for? ▼

Ages 11–22+. YourLife CC has four life stages — Middle School, High School, College, and Young Adult — and content adapts automatically. The same app grows with your child for years.

How does the Parent Hub work? ▼

PIN-protected inside the app. Parents see a Life Score (weighted grade per child), behavior logs, chore verification, grade monitor, screen time controls, and weekly reports — without accessing the child's private journal.

How do multiple kids work? ▼

Every child gets their own PIN and completely separate dashboard. Free plan: 1 child. Family Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime: unlimited children, no extra charge.

What if I cancel my subscription? ▼

You drop to the free tier automatically. Your family's data is never deleted — re-subscribe anytime and pick up where you left off.

Is this faith-based? ▼

YourLife CC includes an optional Faith and Purpose section with 30 devotionals, Bible study content, and a prayer journal. A Faith Mode toggle lets you turn it ON or OFF. The other 29 skill categories are universal — for all families.

What if I'm not satisfied? ▼

30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked. Email [email protected] and we'll refund you immediately — even on the Lifetime plan.
